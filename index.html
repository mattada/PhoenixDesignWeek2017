About PHXDW 2017

Every year, Phoenix Design Week brings together hundreds of creative professionals from across the Southwest for a week­long celebration of design—a series of presentations, workshops, and social events, all curated to inspire, strengthen, and showcase the Arizona design community.

The highlight of Phoenix Design Week is the Method + Madness Conference. Attendees will get two full days of actionable insights from leading professionals, as well as hands­ on education and massive amounts of fiery inspiration.
